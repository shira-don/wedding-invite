Black & dark colors are not allowed.

To keep our garden setting light and cohesive, we kindly ask that no black or dark-toned attire be worn — this includes charcoal, dark navy, deep jewel tones, and other heavy, saturated colors.

Please note: as a firm part of our celebration's theme, guests in black or dark attire will not be granted entry. We truly appreciate your understanding and cooperation. 💜

A Few Notes

Dressing for the Day

The Style

Semi-formal to cocktail attire. Think flowing dresses, elegant separates, and smart suits or barongs in our soft palette.

The Setting

The ceremony is in a garden at Soirée Events Place. December evenings in Indang can turn cool and breezy — a light pastel layer is a lovely idea.
