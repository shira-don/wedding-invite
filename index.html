Our Story

How We Met

A Love Story

We first crossed paths in college, just two electronics engineering students navigating lectures and late-night study sessions. But it wasn't until we were paired together for our thesis that everything changed — long hours in the lab turned into long conversations, and somewhere between the circuit boards and deadlines, we fell in love.


We graduated side by side, took and passed the Electronics Engineering board exam together, and have been inseparable ever since. Eight years, countless adventures, and one unforgettable proposal later, we are overjoyed to invite you to celebrate the next chapter of our story.
